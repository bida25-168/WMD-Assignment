Tips to use when debating

  • Understand the topic first:Debaters tend to rush to find the answers to the case that they are arguing and that is not
    good. It is important to ensure that every term in the motion is defined to the point where each team member understands what is expected of them from the adjudicators.While doing this individually, team memebers should also ensure that there is mutual understanding of the mtions and its requirements between all of them.
  • Don't take things personal:In debates there are times where rude, belittling statements or comments are said as a way to
    show how little the opposing teams points are. Most of these are said in the moment with a competetive mindset and hence some speakers may be offended by it. Nonetheless , as speakers whatever is said to you do not take it literally and remember the points mentioned in this tip.
  • Speak with confidence and audacity:To capture the audience's attention and keep it ensure that the debate speech is:
    • Structured
    • Intrusive of metaphors,analogies
    • Spoken with a convincing tone
    • Actively engaging of the audience
    • Audible and unambiguous
  • Accept feedback:At times after every debate adjudicators tend to help give extran information and tips to debters, it is important
    to not take this as criticism or your efforts aren't being noticed. Rather to see it as an opportunity for improvement and growth as this is one of the hardest things as human we find hard to take in.
